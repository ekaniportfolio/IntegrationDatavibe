# Market Research - Gemini Workspace

This folder is designated for market research performed by Gemini (Google AI).
Gemini has real-time web access and is better suited for:

- Current competitor analysis with live pricing
- Real-time TJM (daily rate) benchmarks by region
- Trending technologies and market movements
- SEO keyword research for content strategy
- Social media trend analysis
